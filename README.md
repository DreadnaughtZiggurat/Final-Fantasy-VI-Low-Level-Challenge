FFVI-Low-Level-Challenge

A low level challenge hack for FFVI.

It's simply the original game with all of the changes below.

A minimalist overhaul of Final Fantasy VI designed for a niche, challenge-focused playstyle.

Core Changes:

    No experience or AP(aka Magic Points after you get access to Espers from Zozo)gained from battles. Terra and Celes are your only magic users.

Magic & Growth Systems:

    Espers no longer provide spell learning lists
    Espers no longer grant stat bonuses as you don't level up
    Items and relics do not teach magic

Battle Mechanics Adjustments:

    Undead bosses are immune to Phoenix Down instant death
    Gau cannot learn the Nightshade Rage

Design Intent:

    Removes standard leveling and magic progression systems
    Emphasizes resource management, strategy, and player knowledge
    Intended for experienced or experimental players seeking a non-traditional run of Final Fantasy VI

Compatibility:

    Built for use with Final Fantasy III (US) Rev 1.1
